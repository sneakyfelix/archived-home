Emily is a net artist from Atlanta. She has a BA in Art History and film from Reed College. She is currently a freelance artist working towards her MFA in art practice at UCxx. You can find her online in one of her many digital homes.

Her reluctance to let go of the past becomes a practice of grief that explores obsession, memory, and treasured found objects. Thus her work often deals with selfhood, relationships, and materiality in the digital age.
