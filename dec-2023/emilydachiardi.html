Emily is a net artist from Atlanta. She has a BA in Art History from Reed College. She is currently a freelance artist working towards her MFA with a concentration in media art and technology at UC Santa Barbara. You can find her online in one of her many digital homes.

Her reluctance to let go of the past becomes a practice of grief that explores obsession, memory, and treasured found objects. Thus her work often deals with selfhood, relationships, and materiality in the digital age.
